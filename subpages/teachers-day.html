TEACHER'S DAY

"Better than a thousand days of diligent study is one day with a great teacher."

Teacher's Day is a special occasion for us to express our heartfelt gratitude and appreciation for the exceptional work that our teachers do day in and day out. Their dedication, passion, and unwavering commitment to educating and nurturing the students deserve to be celebrated in grand style.
We look forward to celebrating this special day with you and expressing our gratitude for our teachers’ exceptional works. Let’s thank them for being an essential part of our educational journey.

When: 5:30 - 8:00 pm, 8th September 2024
Where: Lecture Hall-3
