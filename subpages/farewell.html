FAREWELL NIGHT 2024

As the academic year draws to a close, it gives us great pleasure to extend an invitation to all students, faculties, staff, and other members of our Earth Science community to the upcoming Farewell event on 2nd May.
This event will serve as a momentous occasion to bid farewell to our final year graduating and post-graduating students, celebrating their achievements, contributions, and the invaluable memories they have created.
We eagerly anticipate your presence at the Farewell event.

When: 2nd May, 2024
Where: Room No. 312, ESB-3
