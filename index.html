Welcome to the virtual home of the Earth Science Society @ IIT Kanpur!

The Earth Science Society at IIT Kanpur was established in 2018 with a vision to unite the department of Earth Sciences at IIT Kanpur through various cultural, academic, and sports events.

We intend to do this by-

  • conducting events to bring faculties, staff, and students on the same platform for bonding.
  • building an ecosystem where community members will get various opportunities to develop themselves.
  • fostering a supportive and inclusive community for students and other members.

"Welcome to the Earth Science Society! Here, we blend learning with a vibrant sense of community. Together, we'll delve into the fascinating world of Earth Science while celebrating camaraderie and talent through various activities. We aim to create a space where curiosity thrives, ideas flourish, and connections grow. Whether you're passionate about unravelling Earth’s mysteries, showcasing your creative side, or building friendships that last a lifetime, there’s something here for everyone. Looking forward to your enthusiastic participation!"

-Srijan Mukherjee
President

"Welcome to the Earth Science Society. Let us join hands to explore the wonders of Earth Science while fostering teamwork and talent beyond classrooms through different academic and extracurricular activities like sports, cultural nights, and other engaging events. Together we will make this year enriching, exciting, and unforgettable. Looking forward to your active participation."

-Dr Tajdarul Hassan Syed
Faculty Advisor

"Greetings,
I am excited to welcome you to a journey that blends curiosity, discovery, innovation and deep respect towards each other. Our goal is to cultivate an environment where knowledge thrives and ideas flow. It is our mission to create a welcoming and stimulating environment for all members to engage with each other, whether through field trips, workshops, events or discussion. ESS offers endless opportunities to grow, expand our knowledge and make lasting contributions. We hope each of you finds inspiration here and most importantly, feels empowered to share your unique perspectives. Through our collective wisdom, we can make a meaningful impact on both science and society."

-Dr Anupam Banerjee
Faculty Advisor
